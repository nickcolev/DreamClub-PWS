Distribution Channels

Main channel to be Google (as it is Android app). If in the future an iOS version is developed — Apple channel shall be used. Alternatively, a site with link for download and PayPal payment could be set up.

Support

Cash Flow

Going to rely on the payment mechanisms of Google Play. It requires registration of Google Play Developer Console account with $25 fee. The distribution is 30% for Google (operating costs) vs 70% for the developer. The revenue is paid via Google Wallet.
